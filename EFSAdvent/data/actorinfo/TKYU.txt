TKYU (iron ball (manager))

Variable 1a:
Variable which stops the iron balls

Variable 1a:
0 = alternating left right.
1 = Always centered.
2 = alternating left right,disappear after activating the variable.
3 = Always centered, disappear after activating the variable.

Variable 2:
Random delay until the next ball

Variable 3:
Delay until the next ball

Variable 4a:
0 = South
1 = North
2 = East
3 = West

Variable 4b:
0 = Small balls
1 = Small and big balls
2 = Big balls
