SITM (Goods)

Variable 4:
0 = 300 for mysterious ball
1 = 100 for chicken (Gives 1 Force Gem)
2 = 4 hearts for 100 Force Gem
3 = 300 for Heart Container
4 = 200 for Shovel (and gives 1000 Force Gem)
5 = 300 for Bow (and gives 50 Force Gem)
6 = 600 for Boomerang (and gives 20 Force Gem)
7 = 900 for Fire rod (and gives 150 Force Gem)
