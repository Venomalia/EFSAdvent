FLME (Flame) No GBA sprite

Variable 1:
Trigger by variable X

Variable 2:
Direction
0 = East
1 = West
2 = North
3 = South

Variable 4:
0 = Jet of fire
1 = Jet of fire, off
2 = Jet of fire, when approaching
3 = Jet of fire, on/off time-based
4 = Small ring of fire
5 = Ring of fire
6 = Jet of fire, goes out completely when disabled
