WTSW (Wait switch)
Activates a variable with time delay.

Variable 1:
The local variable number that is triggered or set by this actor after the time has elapsed.

Variable 2:
The local variable number that triggers or activates this actor.

Variable 3:
Strength of the earthquake effects

Variable 4+5+6:
Time until triggering
